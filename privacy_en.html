Privacy Policy

LogSwap / Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this app (the "Service") handles your personal information.

1. Information we collect

Name, bio, handle ID, region (prefecture or country), gender, hashtags, profile image, log video, and Setlog invite IDs. We also collect an anonymous account ID issued automatically for using the Service, and records of your likes, matches, chats, and blocks/reports. Your gender is not shown to other users.

2. Purpose of use

We use the information to create and display profiles, provide the log-exchange features (matching, chat, ID exchange), respond to abuse, and operate and improve the Service. We do not use it beyond these purposes.

3. Storage and external services

Your information is stored on external cloud services used by the Service. Profile and chat data are stored on Supabase (database and authentication), and images and videos on Cloudflare R2, transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. These servers may be located outside your country.

4. Sharing and processors

Except as required by law, we do not provide your information to third parties without your consent. When a log exchange is matched, the information shown on your profile and any Setlog invite ID you disclose are shared with the matched partner. We also entrust data handling to Supabase Inc. and Cloudflare, Inc. for the storage and operation described above.

5. Advertising

The Service offers some free features (such as extra swipes and extra chat slots) in exchange for viewing rewarded video ads. Ads are delivered via Google AdMob. To show, optimize, and measure ads, your device's advertising ID, approximate location, and usage data may be collected and used by Google LLC. See Google's Privacy Policy for details. You can turn off ad personalization in your device settings.

6. Deleting your account and data

You can delete your account and all related data (profile, images, videos, matches, chats, blocks/reports, etc.), including data on our servers, at any time from "Delete account" in the Profile tab. Deletion is permanent.

7. Age requirement

The Service is only for users aged 18 and older.

8. Contact

For inquiries about the handling of personal information, contact the operator at tomokiskriiiabc@gmail.com.
